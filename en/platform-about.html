About Flexberry platform

Flexberry is a technology software platform for professional software development. Allows IT departments of enterprises and development companies to optimize the process of designing, creating and supporting information systems.

Applications Flexberry

  • Comprehensive accounting systems
  • Automation of business processes and workflow
  • Web applications
  • Mobile applications
  • Integration of information systems
  • Analytical systems
  • Geoinformation systems

Advantages of using the Flexberry platform

  • Rapid prototyping of software solutions;
  • Reducing the complexity of the process of developing information systems;
  • Ability to use ready-made software components;
  • Increase the effectiveness of interaction with customers through the use of visual models of systems and processes;
  • Use of the service-oriented approach for the development of the information space of the enterprise;
  • Reducing the cost of maintenance of information systems.

Additional advantages:

  • Update the technological component as part of the subscription;
  • The possibility of training specialists in the practical skills of working with the platform Flexberry;
  • Various options for technical support.

Composition of the Flexberry platform

  • Visual model editors;
  • Generators of program code;
  • Components for the development of software based on various technologies and frameworks:
    • HTML5;
    • ASP.NET;
    • Windows Forms.NET;
    • Microsoft SharePoint;
    • Microsoft Project Server;
    • And others.
  • Universal system integration service.
